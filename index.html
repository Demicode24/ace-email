Have you checked out the American Council on Exercise (ACE) lately? A lot of new and exciting things have happened. Come see for yourself!

We’ve refreshed our products, programs and resources because we want you to have access to current, relevant information that you can use to keep people healthy. From our new brand and website, to our latest courses and research, to our revamped Health Coach Study Program, we have more to offer than ever before!

ACE is so much more than just the leading nonprofit organization that certifies and represents more than 80,000 certified fitness professionals, health coaches and other allied health professionals. We’re your trusted authority in the health and fitness certification industry. Our goal is to shed light on the benefits of a physically-active lifestyle and encourage people to prioritize their health by moving more.

We invite you to see how we’re changing lives one day at a time, and we hope you’ll join in our mission to get people moving.
